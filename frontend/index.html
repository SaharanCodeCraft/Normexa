Detect unseen defects using AI

⚠️ Real-World Challenge

In real-world industries, defects are rare, unpredictable, and constantly changing. Traditional AI systems require labeled defect data — which is expensive, incomplete, and often unavailable. As a result, critical defects can go unnoticed, leading to quality issues, safety risks, and financial loss.

🧠 Our Approach

This system learns only from normal images and builds an understanding of what “good” looks like. Instead of memorizing defects, it detects anomalies by identifying deviations from normal patterns. It highlights suspicious regions and assigns an anomaly score — enabling early detection without requiring labeled defect data.
